# W3C TPAC Accessibility Roles and Responsibilities Mapping (ARRM) Breakout Discussion
Slides to guide the 2025 TPAC breakout group — [Accessibility Roles and Responsibilities Mapping (ARRM)](https://www.w3.org/events/meetings/76eeff8c-9750-4d3e-874b-a32293d6af9c/). 
12 November 2025, 16:15–17:15 Japan Standard Time

## Description  
A discussion about Accessibility Roles and Responsibilities Mapping (ARRM) and its role in helping accessibility evaluation.

### Goal:
Review progress thus far and look to opportunities ahead

### Agenda:
- Review ARRM & its history
- Look at the roadmap for a teams based approach
- Consider the Web Sustainability Guidelines approach to highlight roles
- Consider implications for future WCAG releases
